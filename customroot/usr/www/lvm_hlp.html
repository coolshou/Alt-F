LVM Help

LVM, Logical Volume Manager, allows creating a pool of disk space, called a Volume Group, from several disk partitions and allows creating new devices, called Logical Volumes, from that pool. Disk partitions, called Physical Volumes, can latter be dynamically added or removed to the Volume Group. Logical Volumes and can be dynamically growed or shrinked as needed; to be usable, a filesystem has to be superimposed on the new Logical Volume.
